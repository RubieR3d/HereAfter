Proposal

I propose to create a website that is targeted towards individuals suffering from grief following the loss of a parent or grandparent. I recognize that grief can be an extremely difficult and painful process, and my aim is to provide a source of comfort and encouragement during this challenging time.

The website will feature a range of calming landscapes photographs and inspiration quotes designed to provide hope and support. In addition, I will provide a crisis hotline number, mediative/grounding techniques, and self-care tips to help visitors navigate their emotions and cope with their grief.

To keep the website engaging and up-to-date, I will ensure new quotes and photos are added weekly. The timeline for the website will begin with a beautiful calming landscape photo greeting visitors, followed by an inspiring quote. Visitors will then be provided with a self-care tip and mediative technique, before finally being presented with a crisis hotline.

Please note that the photo content will be sourced from free photos found on the internet, while the quotes will come from religious texts or inspirational leaders. I am confident that the end product will provide a valuable resource for individuals suffering from grief and will be a source of comfort and support.

Thank you for considering our proposal.
